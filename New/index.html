Short bio

I am a computer scientist working on games, machine learning, verification, and dynamical systems. I am a full-time (permanent) researcher at CNRS in LaBRI, Bordeaux (chargé de recherche). I am also a research fellow of The Alan Turing Institute of data science and artificial intelligence in London. I defended my PhD in October 2015.

Curriculum Vitae: English version, French version. Last update: September 2020.

Latest

I am the principal investigator of the DeepSynth project (Momentum 2019 -- 2021) whose goal is to combine formal methods and machine learning for program synthesis.
