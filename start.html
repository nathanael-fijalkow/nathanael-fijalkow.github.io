Hello! I am a computer scientist. My research interests include games, machine learning, verification, and dynamical systems.



I am a full-time (permanent) researcher at CNRS in LaBRI, Bordeaux (chargé de recherche).
I am also a research fellow of The Alan Turing Institute of data science and artificial intelligence in London.

I am the principal investigator of the DeepSynth project (Momentum 2019 -- 2021) whose goal is to combine formal methods and machine learning for program synthesis.

Check out my research blog Games automata play!
